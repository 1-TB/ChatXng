Getting Started

Welcome to ChatXng! Here's a quick guide to help you get started with our application:

  • Go to settings and set your api key to access the chat features.
  • Click on the "Home" link to initiate a conversation with ChatGPT.
  • Use the message input field to type your messages and click "Send" to send them.
